EduVital is a dedicated platform designed to support students in managing their mental health and academic well-being. My goal for this project is to help students thrive both personally and academically, fostering resilience and self-care in a supportive online space.

About

This section aims to align and inform people about my objectives of the study

1. Develop a user-friendly web interface that provides access to mental health resources, tools, and support services.

2. Implement features within the application that promote stress management techniques, relaxation exercises, and mindfulness practices.

3. Create a secure and confidential platform for students to seek guidance from licensed mental health professionals through chat or virtual counseling sessions.

4.Integrate personalized features, such as goal setting, and self-assessment quizzes, to facilitate self-awareness and self-care among users.

5. Collaborate with university counseling centers and mental health experts to ensure the accuracy, relevance, and effectiveness of the resources and interventions offered within the application.

6. Evaluate the usability, effectiveness, and impact of the mobile application through user feedback, surveys, and qualitative assessments to identify areas for improvement and further development.
